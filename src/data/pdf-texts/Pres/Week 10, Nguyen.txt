meidum, unlike painting or sculpture
the medium of CAF (binary code) is

thought and freedom of the
audience. Because it is interactive

and work, they are apart of the work.

A striving player acquires, temporarily, an
interest in winning for the sake of the
struggle. Thus, striving play involves a
motivational inversion from ordinary life.
In ordinary practical life, we pursue the
means for the sake of the ends. But in
striving play, we pursue the ends for the
sake of the means. We take up a goal for
the sake of the activity of struggling for it.
